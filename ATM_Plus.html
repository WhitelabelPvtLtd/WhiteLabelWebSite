Don’t want to manage the cash loading?

PLAN DETAILS:

With this plan White Label maintains ownership of the ATM and loads the machine.

White Label will take care of everything, installation, service, cash loading and monitoring, communications and with the full turnkey solution and you will get a percentage of the surcharge for providing the floor space.

This Plus Plan is best for busy locations whose main concern is having a reliable ATM available for your customers and don’t want any of the responsibility of looking after the ATM.
